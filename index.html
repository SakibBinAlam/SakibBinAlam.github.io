About Me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Florida. I am very fortunate to working with Dr. Shaun Canavan (my advisor) and others in computer vision and pattern recognition research group. Previously, I was an Adjunct Lecturer of Computer Science and Engineering at International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh. I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in August 2016. My undergraduate research advisors were Professor Tanveer Ahsan and Lecturer (on leave) Md. Azher Uddin. I love to learn new things, and to do boxing and to watch soccer.
